Our Story

Saving patients' life through the use of appropriate medical device shouldn't be some luxury that only the rich hospitals can afford. Getting the necessary medical care is a right we all share. However, given the high purchasing and maintenance costs of medical devices, too many regions are deprived of this right.

At Medcycle, we aim to change that - through the power of upcycling and energy harnessing.

Here's our solution


1

Purchase

We purchase usable medical devices from hospitals that are planning on upgrading to newer versions. After careful investigation on their usability and functionalities, we purchase them at a price individually tailored with considerations on the original price, the condition and the type of the device.

2

Renovate

We renovate the purchased devices to fit the needs of rural regions. The renovations we provide not only include ensuring functionalities but also include making the devices less energy-intensive.

3

Re-sell

We sell the renovated medical devices at a cut down cost to hospitals in need. From installation to customer support, our client hospitals enjoy the much-needed medical devices at an affordable cost with lower energy demand.
